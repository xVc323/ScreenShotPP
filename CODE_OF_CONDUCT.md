# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want ScreenShotPP to be a welcoming, respectful, and productive project for everyone.

Project participants are expected to act with empathy, assume good intent, and help keep discussions focused on improving the software and the community around it.

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that helps the project include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Giving constructive feedback and accepting it gracefully.
- Keeping bug reports, feature requests, and reviews focused on the work.
- Acknowledging mistakes and correcting them when needed.

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized language or imagery where it is not relevant to the project.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or deliberately derailing discussions.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior that is harmful to the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers. If the report involves a security-sensitive matter, use the private reporting process described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Reports will be handled with care and as much confidentiality as practical.
